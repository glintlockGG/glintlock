### Room Key: LEVEL 2

#### 30. Shark Suite
- Four-poster bed, shark wallpaper, dead servants. Corpse hanging from silk noose: bulbous fish eyes, silver shark brooch (100 GP), full of corpse flies.
- Toy soldiers: solid silver (300 GP). Servants rise as chaos zombies when touched.
- **Mask of Selachi**: wearer's bones turn to soft cartilage. Squeeze through narrow gaps. -4 to melee/STR.

#### 31. Spider Suite
- White spider-silk curtains, sticky carpet. **Miss Imogen** (25, lovely horror) sits reading.
- Wants to be left alone. Shushes once. Speak again: leaps, reveals spider face, vomits webbing (save vs breath or mute 2d6 hours, burn to remove immediately for d4).

#### 32. Crab Suite
- Red shell tiles. Elderly naked man in tub, red crab chitin skin. Throat torn by motley magpie.
- **Ebony Cane** (red jade octopus handle, sleep in water = euphoric dreams + 1 hp/hour, cannot wake on own).
- 3-in-6 motley magpie hiding in blankets on bed.

#### 33. Beetle Suite
- Dead red priest with dagger in heart (Viola's stiletto, mirror blade, 300 GP).
- White ivory box: save vs spells or cursed d6 hours to desire contents (currently old toenail clippings). Multiple cursed people fight for them.

#### 34. Tiger Suite
- Two women (Lady Julia and Lady Juliet, 55, lovely horrors with tiger faces/claws) mutually stabbing each other (not dead, just clumsy). Want to die by noble hands.
- Nine black cats hiding from rain. One wears collar with **black pearl charm** (paired earrings: telepathic messages 500', 3/day).
- Fireplace: climb to banquet hall (down) or Duke Avito's room (up).

#### 35. Unicorn Suite
- Count and Countess Malvolio (lovely horrors, unicorn horns/hooves) waltz on bed.
- Eight naked dead copies of the couple (created by magic eggs), gored open, hearts/livers eaten.
- Side table: ebony box with 12 dimples, 4 contain **blue eggs** (break on creature = lifeless clone).

#### 36. Falcon Suite
- Dead red priest in overflowing tub. **Professor Marinda Boaj** (35, plague doctor) inspects body.
- Willing to join party. Knows red priests sacrificed eyes/arms for spirit powers. Suspects Vermesh is in keep. Knows Holy Staff of Vitelis can banish a true horror.
- If mask removed: instantly mutates into **plague raven**. If sees corpse: 1-in-6 accidentally turns it to chaos zombie.
- **Poxmaster Mask**: immune to disease, mutation, inhaled effects.

#### 37. Dragon Suite
- Outer wall gone. Every surface crawls with red worms. **Sycorax** (40, red priest) gazes out broken wall.
- Three mangled bodies at her feet. Wants to find Vermesh. Fears Princess Viola.
- **Blue jade chess set** (500 GP). Sycorax animates 3 chaos zombies from servant bodies when fighting.

#### 38. Linen Closet
- 1-in-6 lovely horror gnawing dead servant. Sheets, pillows, towels.

#### 39. Liquor Closet
- 1-in-6 lovely horror gnawing dead servant. Wine, absinthe, rum, gin, brandy, whiskey, vodka.

#### 40. Secret Passages
- Pristine narrow corridors behind panels. Thick burnt candles. Two dead lovely horrors: mutant feathers/beaks, transmuted to black marble, mid-coitus. Incredibly heavy; stepping on them crashes through floor to banquet hall below.

#### 41. Silver Chapel
- Obsidian buttresses, silver details, painted dome ceiling with sea angel Rahavil.
- Six pews with prayer books (five defaced, one intact: 1,000 GP). Silver altar (3,000 GP). **Chaos Idol of Vermesh** (red jade, 5,000 GP, Lawful can't cast/heal in chapel, cursed: bearer saves vs death daily or becomes wyrmling).
- Four inkaran porcelain vases (300 GP each). **Eye of Rahavil** (immune to drowning, still unconscious 2d6 turns).
- **Seal trap**: moving silver Rahavil figurine past seal slams door shut and fills chapel with seawater.

#### 42. Quiet Nook
- Impossibly soft white fur carpets (albino sea snake, 500 GP x 6 bolts). Sky-blue velvet couches.
- Obsidian fountain with eight entwined boas/vipers. Enchanted water: save vs petrification or random transformation (1d6: slender/muscular/voluptuous man/woman).

#### 43. Glass Garden
- Shattered glass ceiling, charred flowers on muddy floor. **Tiny golden box** in mud (blue pearl plays soothing violin, 300 GP).
- d6 bulbs remain (200 GP each): 1-2=black orchid (pain-free intoxication d6 hours), 3-4=starlight jasmine (heightened senses d6 hours), 5-6=moonrise lotus (no sleep, fatigue-free 24 hours).

#### 44. Billiards Room
- Scorched table. Twelve obsidian alcoves with cut sapphires (actually **sapphire scarabs**: swarm if removed).
- Six pockets with nine sapphire billiard balls (250 GP each).
- 2-in-6 floor collapse into banquet hall.
- **Vlad, Boris, Arkady** (scavengers from another island). Practical, amoral. Want maximum treasure. Examining sapphire billiard ball. Loot sack: 3,500 GP.

#### 45. Private Theater
- Burnt remains, frame of puppet theater.
- Leather case: eight lifelike puppets in violet velvet (50 GP each).
- Climb outside to Prince Orsino's room (up) or to falcon/dragon suites (horizontal).

#### 46. Mews
- Melted iron bars on windows. Charred falconer equipment. Seven dead birds. **Lady Isabeau** (mutant falcon) on eighth perch.
- Lazy, never flies. Glowing red eyes, shoots heat rays (d4) at small prey and annoying people. Loyal to calm quiet person who feeds her.

#### 47-48. Stairs
- **47. East Stairs**: down to Level 1. 2-in-6 weary lovely horror coming up.
- **48. South Stairs**: up to Level 3. 2-in-6 wary red priest coming down to find sacrifice.
