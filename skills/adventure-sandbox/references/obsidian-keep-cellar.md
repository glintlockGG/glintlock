### Room Key: CELLAR

#### 1. Boiling Cistern
- Underground reservoir boiling furiously. Waist-deep, 100x100 feet. Heated by approaching lava (d8 on touch, 1/round in water). Blinding steam (visibility zero, no fire).
- Body at water's edge with officer's badge. **Officer Badge** (immune to fear, command up to 10 humans to stop/flee/be silent 1/day).
- Hidden: locked black iron hatch to tunnel out to Grassy Hillside.
- If touched, body sits up and screams until head falls off. 1d6 chaos zombies animate each round until all 13 bodies animated.

#### 2. Blazing Barracks
- Long vaulted chamber. Jagged chasm full of lava. Iron bunk beds and chests.
- Each turn: 3-in-6 cinder shrimp from lava, 2-in-6 chance 2d6 corpses rise as chaos zombies.
- Chests: sky blue uniform, blanket, amulet, empty satchel, burnt rations, dagger, melted soap, mirror, canteen.
- 50 burnt soldier corpses. Letter: "my sweet Gianni... the masters grow more monstrous by the hour..."
- **NOTE**: Cellar slowly filling with lava. If Level 1 doors opened, water spills down cooling lava to rock for 3d6 hours.

#### 3. Arctic Armory
- Ice crystals in air despite nearby lava. Tall wooden racks with black-steel weapons.
- Lava enters in 1 turn, ignites racks.
- **Nechrome Weapons** (rare starmetal): 2 daggers, 1 rapier, 1 two-handed sword, 1 spear, 10 arrows. Cause freezing wounds (1/round until warmed), freeze 10'x10'x10' water on contact (1/day).

#### 4. Crooked Chapel
- Shattered floor, leaning sharply. Stone statue of Saint Eryn.
- Under bench: braided silver wedding ring (25 GP).
- Kneel and cut hand on bronze sword: saint asks "What do you fear more than death?" Answers get +2 bonus near that fear for d6 hours, then -2 penalty for d6 hours.

#### 5. East Stairs
- Spiral up to Level 1. Iron door locked (keeping prisoners down). Opening floods blazing barracks, cooling lava.

#### 6. Cell 1
- **Percy** (35, ex-stable boy). Gorgeous, no mutations. Locked up because Princess Bianca had a crush 20 years ago. Blessed: cannot be attacked by red priests or visceral corruptions. Wanders when not watched.

#### 7. Cell 2
- **Byron** (30, charming thief). Stained green coat. Locked up for trying to steal an amber orb from Duchess. Lava filling cell, d6 hours until he passes out.
- **Crimson Catgut** (red cord): wearer immune to disease, deafness, blindness.
- If freed: follows party until he sees escape chance.

#### 8. Cell 3
- Three women's skeletons with rune skulls (spirit/summon). Smash a skull: summons ghost witch who answers one question or performs one task. Corpse flies swarm under bones.

#### 9. Cell 4
- Gray sheet hides tunnel to narrow rock ledge 50' above waves. Two corpses on rocks below.
- Seagull on ledge wearing **Sapphire Ring** (puts on = turn into seagull; remove = reverts to elderly woman Gertrid who knows bread recipes).

#### 10. Cell 5
- White weasel nursing two kittens in nest of human bones (prisoner devoured by weasel). Loyal to whoever feeds them first.

#### 11. Cell 6
- **Vertex** (900-year-old Ghiran machine priest). Slender, rusty, blue robe. Gentle, timid. Glowing blue **Soulstone** in chest (18 INT, 4-in-6 identify, phobic of chaos). 3-in-6 flees at chaos mutations.

#### 12. Cell 7
- **Helena** (25, ducal soldier). Tall, muscular, sky blue uniform, vulture talon hand. Wants to fight red-robed freaks. Bad at loyalty/stealth. If freed, runs off to fight (3-in-6 gets herself killed).

#### 13. Cell 8
- **Baroness Oklavia** (45, lovely horror). Sparkling violet gown, lace fan. Seductive, playful, mocking. Insect mandible mouth behind mask. Owns **blue pearl key**.
- If freed: goes to banquet hall and drinks. Lovely horrors don't attack while she's nearby. When drunk, forgets party and acts as standard lovely horror.

#### 14. Black Pit
- 10' wide, 50' deep. Lava reaches in 1 hour.
- **Pavnutia** (2,200-year-old space vampire alchemist). Gray skin, no legs (chopped off by Duchess Forza), teeth extracted. Currently powerless.
- If fed human blood (15 damage worth): regrows legs/teeth, regains full power (AC 17, HD 9, immune to non-magical weapons, many abilities), slaughters nobility, establishes herself as Duchess of Night.
- **Vampire's Dress** (cosmoth silk, immunity to light-based dangers).
