### Room Key: THE BEACH

#### 1. Cave Hut
- Ship's cabin in shallow cave. **Sandro** (35, fisherman, hates mutant servants) and **Nora** (10, sarcastic, wants monster stories).

#### 2. Overturned Dinghy
- **Ivo** (45, clam digger, ready to sail away). Needs an intact sail. Knows about the tunnel under the western wall leading to dungeons (Grassy Hillside). Needs a black iron key.

#### 3. Lava Stream
- Bright yellow lava stream from the Keep across beach into water. 10' wide, foul hot steam.
- 2-in-6 chance of **cinder shrimp swarm**.

#### 4. Stilt Hut
- Tiny wooden hut on 10-foot stilts. **Oskor** (70, toothless, spear-fisher).
- Drunk, slept through red storm. Misses daughter Velia.
- Give toy boat: he gives up drinking, gives away his Requia rum (3 portions, each = 8 hours sleep).
- Don't give toy: dies of alcohol poisoning within a day.

#### 5. Canvas Tent
- Kettle crab attacking woman with baby. **Clio** (25, palace maid, red scales on hand) and baby **Rico**.
- Claims she saw dark stains in Duke Avito's laundry for months (he was sacrificing animals).

#### 6. Black Shell
- Slimy barnacle-encrusted shell in wet sand. Silver rune "Angelfire."
- Unexploded angelfire shell from the fleet. 5' diameter, 100 lbs. Handle gently. One strike = boom (6d6 damage, 30' radius).

#### 7. Sandy Mound
- 30' mound stinking of salt and decay. Six large kettle crabs (territorial).
- Contains 72 dead sailors. Treasure: 12 daggers, yellow uniforms, 1 angelfire grenade, 109 GP.

#### 8. Driftwood Shack
- Two families of palace servants. Led to safety by Prince Orsino. Children: Aldo (Faekin ear), Lena (goat eye), Gino (fang). Women: Flora (pig nose), Berta (horn). Mutations started a year ago when Duke Avito began fighting with his wife.

#### 9. Gold Tent
- **Hamza** (25, scholar from Radiant Vitelis). Black eye from run-in with scavengers.
- Wants red glass from the red tree (Area 13). Too scared to go near it. Offers **Hamza's Gloves** (safely touch any object: hot, cold, magical, acid, lava, fire, etc.).

#### 10. Filthy Tent
- Bear traps (d6) in sand around tent, 2-in-6 to trigger within 15'.
- **Dmitri** and **Lazar** (30, scavengers/thieves). Inside: locked chest with 1,241 FP, 47 white pearls (2,350 GP), 14 cutlasses, two red pearl earrings (500 GP), two angelfire grenades, five boots, three peg legs, one stuffed parrot.

#### 11. Stone Arch
- Ancient tomb of the magician Prospero. 50' cliff climb required.
- Markings in olde common: "Keep out! Danger! It was very hard to kill him! Leave him dead! Please!"
- Sarcophagus (skull missing, taken by Duchess Forza 10 years ago). Platinum ring (200 GP) and **Arcane Eye** (Prospero's preserved eye, touch to object/creature for sarcastic identification 1/day).

#### 12. Black & Red Tent
- **Ezio** (40, bodyguard, cut off his own ear to remove mutation) and **Magdalena** (35, delusional merchant lady, porcelain mask hiding insane smile with beaver teeth). Claims Duke Avito will restore everything. Wants her stolen red pearl earrings (offers 1,000 GP reward, has no money).

#### 13. Red Tree
- Twisted red glass pillar (fulgurite). Black skeleton frozen inside. Silver circlet on skull.
- 30' from pillar: muted screaming.
- **Orsino's Circlet** (1,000 GP). Touch it: dead Prince Orsino begs to break glass and bury his bones, tells his story: "I blame the red priests. Find my sister Bianca!"
- When placed in its case in Prince Orsino's room (Area 50): summons healing sylph (d6 hp, cure poison/disease, 1/day). While worn: +1 WIS, disables trap at Bianca's room.

#### 14. Buried Cottages
- 50 stone cottages half-buried in cooled lava. No signs of life.

#### 15. Roaring Bonfire
- Two dozen survivors around driftwood fire. Yellow jackets (stranded sailors) and leather smocks (palace servants with mutations).
- **Source of retainer characters and henchmen.**
- Servants are oddly serene, glad to be free. Willing to help for coin and passage off the island.
