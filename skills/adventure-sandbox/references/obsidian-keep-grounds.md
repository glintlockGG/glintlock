### Monster Stat Blocks

Use these stat blocks when running creatures specific to this adventure. For standard monsters (skeletons, etc.), reference the bestiary skill.

#### Skeleton (Sailor/Soldier)
- **LV 1**, HP 4, AC 12, ATK 1 weapon +1 (1d6)
- Undead: immune to poison, charm, hold, sleep. Make no noise until they attack.

#### Harbor Shark
- **LV 2**, HP 9, AC 12, ATK 1 bite +1 (1d6+2)
- Blood scent 300'. Feeding frenzy: always attack when blood detected, no morale checks.

#### Vulgrane (Large black flying saurian)
- **LV 2**, HP 9, AC 12, ATK 1 talon +1 (1d6)
- Dive attack: first attack +2 to hit. Soaring doom: 1-in-6 on hit to snatch and toss 30' (2d6 damage).
- Bile sac worth 30 GP.

#### Kettle Crab
- **LV 2**, HP 9, AC 14, ATK 2 pincers +1 (2d4)
- Pincer loot: 2-in-6 chance to recover a pincer worth 35 GP.

#### Keep Soldier
- **LV 2**, HP 9, AC 14 (black chainmail), ATK 1 guisarme +1 (1d10)

#### Cinder Shrimp (Swarm)
- **LV 3**, HP 13, AC 12, auto-damage: 2 hp (armored) / 4 hp (unarmored)
- Immune to fire/heat. Only harmed by cold and sleep spells. Escaping: half damage for 1 round while removing.

#### Obsidian Skeleton
- **LV 2**, HP 7, AC 12 (obsidian shards), ATK 1 gladius +1 (1d6+1) or spear (1d6+3)
- Undead: immune to poison, charm, hold, sleep.

#### Ghastly Soldier
- **LV 2**, HP 7, AC 14, ATK 1 sword +1 (1d8)
- Stench: 5' radius, save vs poison or 1d6. Undead immunities.

#### Flailing Mass
- **LV 5**, HP 22, AC 14, ATK 1 flailing limbs +5 (2d6)
- Insane Whispers: 5' radius, save vs spells or 1d6 and spend next turn staggering.

#### Ash Louse
- **LV 4**, HP 18, AC 14, ATK 1 bite +4 (2d4)
- Bitter spray: every 3rd round, 15' cone, save vs breath or 2d6 poison.

#### Ember Puck
- **LV 1**, HP 4, AC 14, ATK 1 claw +4 (1d6) or heat metal (1d6)
- Death burst: 5' radius, save vs breath or 2d6. Resist fire (half). Vulnerable to cold (double).

#### Chaos Zombie
- **LV 2**, HP 9, AC 11, ATK 2 claws +1 (1d4)
- Chaotic Flesh: mutates when harmed (1d6): 1=feathers (double MV), 2=scales (+2 AC), 3=antlers (charge 1d6), 4=extra arm (extra claw), 5-6=talons (damage to d6).
- Undead immunities.

#### Lovely Horror
- **LV 3**, HP 13, AC 14, ATK 2 weapons +2 (1d6)
- Seduce: hear voice, save vs spells or defend/obey. Mutate: grow monstrous (3-in-6 to increase damage to d8, then d10, d12).
- Noxious Perfume: 5' radius, save vs poison or -2 damage.
- Mutations (1d8): 1=falcon, 2=crab, 3=spider, 4=shark, 5=beetle, 6=tiger, 7=unicorn, 8=dragon.

#### Red Priest
- **LV 2**, HP 9, AC 14 (hidden chainmail), ATK 1 spirit claw (1d8)
- Spirit Claw: target paralyzed 1 turn (save vs paralysis). Spirit Eyes: perfect vision always.
- Phantom Parry: halve melee damage (3/day). Phantom Grab: seize melee weapon (1/day).
- Immune to mind-affecting spells. Chaotic Death: 2-in-6 erupts into visceral corruption.

#### Ravenous Wyrmling
- **LV 3**, HP 13, AC 10, ATK 1 bite +5 (1d10) or thrash (1d8, all within 10')
- Slam: latch on, lift, slam down. Target prone, drops held items (save vs death).
- Immune to fire/lightning. Slippery ground nearby (save vs paralysis to move).

#### Visceral Corruption
- **LV 4**, HP 18, AC 15, ATK 2 claws +3 (1d8) or bile spray or sinew snare
- Bile Spray: 20' cone (d6). Sinew Snare: save vs paralysis or prone/immobilized.
- Horrific Aspect: save vs paralysis or unable to act 1d4 rounds.
- Climbs walls/ceilings. Cutting attacks cause it to split into 2 HD (9 hp) copies.

#### Motley Magpie (Assassin)
- **LV 3**, HP 13, AC 14, ATK 2 claws +3 (1d6) or garrote
- Garrote: target choked, 1d6/round, save vs death each round or die.
- Acrobat: when harmed, cartwheel 30' away taking no damage (2/day).

#### Corpse Flies (Swarm)
- **LV 3**, HP 13, AC 12, auto-damage: 2 hp (armored) / 4 hp (unarmored)
- Only harmed by fire, sleep, smoke, area attacks. Burrow into flesh.

#### Scavenger
- **LV 1+1**, HP 5, AC 14 (breastplate), ATK 1 weapon +1 (1d8)
- Grenado: d8 + blinding flash + ignites. Desperado: morale check each time harmed, flees on failure.

### Room Key: KEEP GROUNDS

#### 1. South Gate
- 50' black walls, mangled iron gate (never opens again). Elegant black carriage crushed under debris.
- Dead gentleman: white porcelain mask, snuff box of **Red Orchid Dust** (giggle uncontrollably 2d6 turns, infectious), **Blue Pearl Key** (opens blue pearl door Area 22), letter from Duke Avito to fleet: "Surrender or be devoured by the conqueror worm!"

#### 2. Standing Tower
- Round black tower, charred roof. Open archway entrance.
- Interior: spiral stairs, dead soldiers in black armor. Seven black longbows, seven breastplates, 37 arrows, one **black iron key** (opens Grassy Hillside).

#### 3. Fallen Tower
- Rubble around smoking volcanic vent. Bright lava oozes down to beach.
- Can climb rubble to enter grounds. Beware searing heat and toxic fumes (save vs poison or 2d6 turns coughing, 10' visibility).

#### 4. Floating Cottage
- Stone cottage torn from wall by red storm, floating 30' up, slowly tumbling.
- Residual charge: one touch = 1d6 shock, removes charge.
- Interior: cobbler's tools, nine shoes, **Black Leather Jacket** (all reaction rolls are coin tosses: heads = total success, tails = horrific failure).

#### 5. Stables
- Volcanic rock covers floor. Seven horse skeletons in ashen corners.
- **Skull Horse**: shining black horse with white skull head. Friendly, eats ash and pebbles. Undead (no sleep/breath, immune to poison/disease). MV 240' (80').

#### 6. Cages
- Four iron cages. Three open and empty. **Mutant Hound** in fourth: rattle tail, shark teeth, third eye. Will accept one new master (whoever pets it first). LV 4, HP 18, AC 12, bite +3 (2d4 + bleed 1d3/round).

#### 7. Armored Cottage
- **Silvio** (45, blacksmith, mutant crab-claw hand). Paranoid survivalist, thinks party are ghosts.
- Has **Silvio's Armor** (black plate, immune to fire and lightning), **Silvio's Cleaver** (1d8+2, shatters on attack roll of 1), **Silvio's Chain** (10' reach, 2d4+2, wraps wielder on attack roll of 1).

#### 8. White Garden
- Razor wire fence. Mutant blood-orange trees: produce 1 orange/hour, safe to eat (restore 1d6 hp), rot in d6 hours. White pumpkins full of worms (1d6 if eaten).
- **Alma** (25, gardener, bone-white skin/hair, covered in white leaves). Can speak to plants, very strong. Wants to stay. Heard inhuman scream during red storm.

#### 9. Shed
- Three metal vats with dark water, ash, algae, rotting clothing. Foot-long **red centipedes** in the scum (LV 1, bite 1d4 + poison: horribly sick 10 days, save vs poison, half speed only).

#### 10. Kitchen
- Spotless kitchen. **Durante** (20, thin, webbed green fingers). Thinks party are Duke/Duchess. Serves them. Knows Duchess Forza was going to rule the seven cities but Duke Avito was jealous.
- Pantry: eating food, 1d10 roll of 1 = mutation (1d4: 1=snake tongue, 2=termite mandibles, 3=beetle eyes, 4=small third arm).

#### 11. Burnt Rooms
- Ten scorched servant rooms. Charred corpses. Letter: "My darling Dafne, soon I'll have enough saved to take you away..."

#### 12. Main Entrance
- Tall black doors locked, iron bars. Difficult to push open (total Strength 50). Opens into flooded foyer (Area 15 Keep).
- Smoldering crack around entrance with lava. **Ash louse** bathing in lava, attacks anything jumping over crack. Opening doors spills water down, boils ash louse to death.

#### 13. Grassy Hillside
- West of keep, hidden in tall grass: black iron hatch. Locked (black iron key).
- Narrow tunnel to second locked hatch, opens into boiling cistern (Area 1 Cellar).
