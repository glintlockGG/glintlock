### Room Key: LEVEL 3

#### 49. South Stairs
- Down to Level 2. 2-in-6 scared red priest fleeing Princess Viola.

#### 50. Prince Orsino's Room
- Obsidian door in black iron frame of dozens of iron hands. Handle twisted into screaming face (no longer works). Magically reinforced (12 turns/person to break down).
- Bed: bedposts are wooden vipers (THAC0 17, d4 + poison d4/round). Sheets: hissing, strangle (THAC0 17, d4 + entangle). Burn to destroy.
- Desk: diary (bored prince wanted to be a pirate), 324 GP. **Obsidian box**: place Orsino's circlet to summon healing sylph.
- Wardrobe: nautical charts (500 GP), sextant, compass, spyglass. Ships in bottles (12 toys, silver "water" = 100 GP each).
- Bath: cursed, breaks free, gallops around. Paintings: cursed, fire tiny broadsides (d4). Hidden experimental ship designs on backs (1,000 GP).

#### 51. Princess Viola's Room
- Locked (white iron key from banquet hall). Magically reinforced.
- Bed: tiny red worms. Ebony legs partly clear glass. Desk: lists of rooms/names crossed out, 619 GP. **Obsidian box** for Viola's circlet.
- Wardrobe: 19 swords, 34 daggers, 11 maces, 7 hand axes, 4 flails.
- Papers: ineffective prayer scrolls. Walls partly clear glass with worm ant-farm inside.

#### 52. Princess Bianca's Room
- Door ajar. **Trap**: obsidian needles at threshold (THAC0 17, 2d6). Wearing any royal circlet disarms. Scorpion tail handle stings (d6).
- Four-poster bed: bedposts twisted into giant fingers, scuttles like spider. Ragdoll enchanted toy on sheets.
- Porcelain dolls everywhere: eyes follow party, giggle, at scariest moment leap onto party. After 3 rounds burst open spilling thousands of worms forming **6 ravenous wyrmlings**.
- Desk: 3 GP, **obsidian box** for Bianca's circlet.

#### 53. Duchess Forza's Room
- Obsidian and gold furniture. Ancient books (dead languages, sea monsters, spellcraft: 1,200 GP). Scrolls of lightning bolt, phantasmal force, magic missile, levitate.
- Five sphere-sized alcoves: insert amber sphere to activate (bedside: dim light/music; desk: amber lamps/fan; mirror: see harbor through colossus eyes; balcony: warm shade/lavender; oil portrait: vanishes to reveal **true vault**).
- **True vault**: dozens of locked iron chests: 9,000 GP in sapphires, 4,000 GP in gold currencies.
- 23 amber spheres (charged with storm energy, smash for d6 in 20' radius).
- **Duchess Forza**: charred corpse, chest exploded, worms in sockets. Gold crown, white staff. Can speak.
  - Asks: "Did I destroy the fleet? Where are my children?"
  - If convinced she saved the palace and children are fine: gratefully crumbles to dust.
  - If told the truth: screams, red lightning destroys her (2d6 to all nearby, save vs breath half). Staff flies into sea.
- **Forza's Staff** (bones of dead magicians including Prospero's skull, immune to lightning, summon storm clouds, azure lightning d6 to 5 targets or 5d6 to 1, 1/day).
- **Forza's Crown** (solid gold, black pearls/sapphires, doubles range/duration/power of any spell 1/day).

#### 54. Duke Avito's Room
- Door: magically reinforced, locked. **Magic trap**: dozen ghost-worms scream and bite at threshold (1d12, save vs paralysis or unable to move 1d6 turns). Fang handle (very sharp).
- Iron plates on windows. No furniture. Thousands of cracks. Red blankets, lit red candles, blood-red runes on tiles. Three chanting red priests (Godric, Harlow, Ravenna). **Bianca's circlet** on ground (1,000 GP, +1 CHA, disables Bianca's room trap).
- Bath: empty, caked in dried mud. **Enchanted (Chaos) portal** to private sanctum (Area 55).

#### 55. Private Sanctum
- Domed chamber above Level 3, no doors/windows. Only accessible via teleporting bath in Duke Avito's room (or climbing through gash in roof).
- **Duke Avito**: dead, right arm torn off. Black robes, golden ruby chains (600 GP), **Avito's Crown** (gold, blue pearls/sapphires, 3,000 GP). Ring of 13 keys (every door in keep + Bianca's chains).
- Dead animals (chickens, goats, stabbed in heart over a year of sacrifice attempts).
- **Princess Bianca**: alive! Blood-soaked dress, pale, trembling. Crystal knife in her still-beating heart. Chained to floor.
  - "Don't touch the knife..."
  - If crystal knife touched: unseen creature bites off an arm (2d6 hp)
  - If Bianca touched: become able to see Vermesh (huge ethereal worm rising from her body)
  - If knife pushed in: Bianca dies, **Vermesh materializes** fully. Character receives Voidflesh Blessing.
  - If knife pulled out: Vermesh bites off arm and vanishes. Bianca can be healed.
- **Crystal Knife**: ancient magic blade (tooth of a seraphim), can pierce anything, only 1d4 damage.
- **Voidflesh Blessing**: skin closes over eyes (blind). Gain: sense shapes/movement 60' by hearing. Can consume any material as food. Immune to disease.
