### Room Key: LEVEL 1

#### 15. Flooded Foyer
- Cold murky water knee-deep on black marble. Two corpses in corner: lovely horrors with crab mutations, red chitin-skin. Corpse flies burst from mouths when disturbed.

#### 16. Drowned Ballroom
- Shattered windows, water on black marble. Crystal chandeliers shaped like human hands. Warped cellos/violins scream when touched. Dozens of dead lovely horrors.
- Each turn: 2-in-6 random body rises as chaos zombie, 2-in-6 vomits corpse flies.
- Three lovely horrors looting dead for jewelry. Want servants and luxury. Fear poverty and work. Love Duchess Forza.
- **Blue pearl key** on one, four blue pearl necklaces (200 GP), three family brooches (100 GP).

#### 17. Banquet Hall
- Long granite dining table. Dishes for a hundred guests. Rotten fish/fowl covered in red worms.
- **Princess Viola** by fireplace, staring at dead red priestess. Glass-clear skin, tiny red worms visible.
- Viola wants all red priests dead. Asks about Orsino and Bianca. Attacks anyone wearing Orsino's circlet.
- Dead red priestess: amulet of Vermesh (300 GP).
- Fireplace: climb to tiger suite (Level 2) and Duke Avito's room (Level 3).
- **Viola**: AC 16, 4 hp (glass cannon), 4 rapier attacks +5 (1d8). When killed, bursts into 3 ravenous wyrmlings.
- Items: **Viola's Rapier** (250 GP), **Viola's Circlet** (1,000 GP, +1 DEX, disables Bianca's room trap), **white iron key** (Viola's room).

#### 18. Rippling Gallery
- Rain blows in. Score of huge oil paintings dripping. Bronze sculptures. Person trapped under center sculpture.
- Paintings: approach within 10', painted figures lunge to smother (save vs paralysis or blinded/suffocating 1d4/round, 20' range).
- Golden frames: 20 of them, 20 lbs each, 250 GP each.
- **Yorick** (30, servant). Left arm fused to bronze peacock. Will drown when water rises. Helped Duchess Forza open Prospero's tomb 10 years ago. Dies = immediately becomes chaos zombie.

#### 19. Sodden Library
- Empty bookshelves, thousands of torn pages in sodden heap. Glistening black spatters defy gravity.
- **Living Ink** (sentient). Communicates by drawing pictures. Wants escape (fears water). Carried in dry book/bottle = loyal, 2-in-6 answers questions about red priests/Duke Avito/Duchess. If denied = attacks (LV 4, HP 18, AC 13, 3 smother +7, 1d10).
- Two servants (Gio and Irma, 55) beating red priest **Uther** demanding he cure mutations.
- Uther has **Silver Book of Hannuviel** (immune to charms, detect lies 1/week, contains road to Carrion Vale).
- Scroll heap: scroll of read languages, floating disc, read magic.

#### 20. Scorched Solarium
- Soaring windows, shifting panes. Each turn: 2-in-6 scorching beam cuts across room (save vs breath or d6).
- **Gold Globe** (30 lbs, 1,000 GP, locked, contains three bottles of Dorona wine: drinkers highly suggestible and flammable, 500 GP each).
- Woman on fainting couch: mutated into sticky ooze. If touched, sticks and engulfs (1d4/round, needs fire/acid, 10 damage to dissolve).

#### 21. Menagerie
- Wrought-iron cages along walkway. Dead red priest on path.
- **Cage 1**: Aellope (ancient bitter harpy). Wants freedom, meat, revenge on Duchess Forza. Deceive song.
- **Cage 2**: Phoenix (fire-bird). Hawk-sized, sparkling. Wise old man voice. Willing companion. Revives from ashes.
- **Cage 3**: Ligeia (siren). Beautiful, fish tail. Sullen. If returned to sea, sings reducing all lovely horrors to half hp. 500 fish scales worth 25 GP each.
- **Cage 4**: Galini (wingless blue dragon). Cat-sized, playful. Shy girl voice. Acid breath.
- **Cage 5**: Sharlo (orb weaver spider). Clever, devious. Writes messages in webbing. Claims loyalty, actually betrays party.
- **Cage 6**: Krios (rubbish sphinx). White goat with old man head and stunted wings. Tries riddles, always confused. Harmless.

#### 22. Blue Pearl Door
- Circular door with dozens of blue pearls. Requires blue pearl key (multiple sources: South Gate, Cell 8, Council Chamber).
- Without key: white lightning 2d6 to all within 10'.
- 60 pearls (100 GP each, 1 hour + DEX check to remove 3d6 intact).
- Inside: false vault. 90 ebony boxes with sapphires. Actually hibernating **sapphire scarabs** (swarm when removed from vault).

#### 23-26. Closets
- **23. Cleaning Closet**: 1-in-6 lovely horror humming nursery rhyme. Mops, buckets, brooms.
- **24. Lighting Closet**: 1-in-6 lovely horror gnawing dead servant. Lanterns, oil, candles.
- **25. Coat Closet**: 1-in-6 lovely horror piling dead rats. Fur coats, rain coats, cloaks, hats.
- **26. Tool Closet**: 1-in-6 lovely horror lecturing maggots. Hammers, nails, saws, rope, ladder.

#### 27. Servant Passages
- Narrow single-file corridors in walls. 2-in-6 chance of d6 chaos zombies.
- 1-in-6 foot falls through floor (stuck 1 turn, STR check). Terrified servants fused into walls: kicking leg, clawing arm, darting eye, silent wailing mouths.

#### 28. Council Chamber
- Massive circular table with wooden model of Isla Cineria. Secret button on miniature lighthouse: hidden drawer with 20 scorpion oil cigars (hallucinogenic, 1,000 GP) and **blue pearl key**.
- Two lovely horrors (**Sir Tybalt** with moth eyes/antenna, **Sir Valentine** with iguana flesh) shouting and throwing things. Know Duchess Forza is in her suite upstairs.
- Dead red priest under table. Sodden papers: decrees from Duke Avito ("All must worship Vermesh").
- Toy boxes: handling figures = save vs spells or compelled d6 hours to respond "Yes, I think that's a really good idea" to any suggestion.

#### 29. East Stairs
- Spiral up to Level 2 and down toward cellar (iron door, locked).
