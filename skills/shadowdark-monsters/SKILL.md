# Monster Attributes

- **AC** — Armor Class. **HP** — Hit Points (LV × d8 + CON mod, min 1; or use average listed).
- **ATK** — Attacks per turn, with bonus and damage. **MV** — Movement (near unless noted).
- **S, D, C, I, W, Ch** — Stat modifiers (not scores).
- **AL** — Alignment (L/N/C). **LV** — Level.
- **Dark-adapted:** All non-humanoid monsters. Ignore darkness penalties. Blinding/deafening still hinders them.
- **Treasure:** Wandering monsters have 50% chance of carrying treasure.
